Brainstorm & sketching Kit

Aylin Kavrokov · Design
Ling Zhou · Project Management

A friendly, expandable and modular tool kit tailored for our strategists and designers. It's used everyday in strategy meetings and during product developement process.

We built the kit with these top requested features in mind:

1. Desktop & mobile device templates with various sizes( small for quick flows; medium for layout options; large for low-fidelity wireframes

2. Easily sharable and printable

3. Quick bullet lists for capturing meeting notes
